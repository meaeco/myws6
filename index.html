Assumptions or theoretical empathy aren’t enough to conceive. Move forward with real and proven data. Observe, practice, test experiments and their effects, confront reality and its stakes.
I conceive systems (devices, services, strategy, etc.) through the understanding of the experience you want to work on, its users, as well as all the actors concerned by it, in order to propose a sustainable service for all.
Are you a company, a start-up, a community, an association, a collective, men and women who carry an ideology of individual and collective better living? Are you working to build and develop an alternative form of coexistence? I can help you with "how to do it".

My practice

Designing through analysis and experimentation

I design your services and their interactions, I script and prototype the sequence of events and actions to be implemented (whether interface, product, space, graphics), based on "Thinking" and "Doing" studies of the experience. I read and practice reality in order to understand it as well as possible (issues, effects, lived experiences, etc.). The social and humanities sciences tools are used for analysis — interviews, observations, etc. — while those borrowed from the applied arts are used for experimentation — matter integration, prototyping, creation, interdisciplinarity.

Open up and explore the possibilities

Reading and practicing the experience also contribute to an exploration process, they open doors to the discovery of new possibilities.

Moving towards transition

In the midst of an environ-mental crisis, we need to think differently about design, public service, management and consumption practices. Aware of the finiteness of this world's resources and noting that many individuals live outside themselves, the designer must think of systems as steps towards new more sustainable conditions of existence and co-existence, for a better individual and collective life.
