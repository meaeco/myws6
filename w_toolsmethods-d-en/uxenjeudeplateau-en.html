Demand

For an urban planning project, we wanted to communicate about the experience lived of the individuals using the intervention area in question. Our aim was to show different aspects of a journey, for us but also for the whole project team, both directional and emotional, for example. To be able to report and communicate the lived experience observed.

Deliverable

The game board allows us to approach and visualize the large number of factors impacting the experience of the course while representing the part of "chance" that influences this same experience.

Our observations and immersions have revealed the difficulty of transcribing the experiences of our potential users, so many and so different are these experiences. Empirical analysis shows that on a single trip there are a multitude of factors influencing the user's experience. The same trip presents different hazards, events such as weather, noise pollution, encounters, number of remaining seats, etc. The same trip presents different hazards, events such as weather, noise pollution, encounters, number of remaining seats, etc. Each hazard plays a role in the emotions felt and the experience lived during the trip. Diversity seemed to us to be one of the essential elements to talk about our trip.

We proposed an alternative to communicate more quickly with other people involved in the project.
